Can I bring my own personal trainer to the gym?

No. Our own experienced, certified personal trainers would love to help you reach your goals or individual needs!

How old do you have to be to work out in the gyms?

A parent or guardian can sign for a child at the age of 12 years, but the guardian must be with them when working out. Once member reaches 16 years of age, no supervision is required.
